Don Stephenson

Founder

Don Stephenson

My information technology experience includes both management and technical delivery, encompassing every significant deployment environment: mainframes, mini's, client/server, mobile devices, embedded systems and cloud services. I am a solution architect, full stack developer and hardware/software integration engineer. My coding experience encompasses proficiency in over 20 languages with high skill in C, C++, SQL and JavaScript. The scope of my project experience includes business, science, factory automation, retail,gi healthcare, biomedical and entertainment domains.

A few notable projects and achievements include:

  • Architect, lead developer and project manager of the Kelly Blue Book web site.
  • Architect, coder and integration engineer of an electronic health records system for a U.S hospital.
  • Architect and developer of a mobile police reporting application for a major U,S, city.
  • Framework integration engineer on procurement management system for county of Orange, CA.
  • Solution architect and signal processing engineer on AI based automated sonar attendant.

Darrell Denlinger

Co-Founder

Darrell Denlinger

A veteran of the software industry, I have worked at the smallest startups and the largest corporations and everything in between. I love technology but I also love solving business problems. The most fun is in the application of the right tools to solve a busines-critical problem and having impact on the business.

In my previous job at Netflix I was a developer and manager of the team that built the automated encoding platform. We were responsible for most of the media processing at Netflix and the storage for that data. This work was high scale in many dimensions, and mission critical, so it had to be robust.

I have also worked in diverse areas such as digital mapping, astronomy, advertising, software tools, legal document search and network monitoring. I prefer breadth over depth, but I also never reach my limit of learning enjoyment in any area.

David Walker

Member

David Walker

David Walker is one of the original pioneers of the global coworking movement and has been a leader in the industry for 15 years. Two years before WeWork was founded, David co-founded and co-operated one of the first 50 coworking spaces in the world in 2008 in Austin. Today there are 26,000+ spaces globally.

Currently, David holds multiple roles: Founding Partner of OpenWork Agency, a future of work consultancy for hybrid organizations; Chief Collaboration Officer at CoworkingConsulting.com, a coworking consultancy helping entrepreneurs & real estate developers build coworking spaces; Board Member/Co-Founder of xCommunity.org, a hub for AI enthusiasts; and, most recently, Director of Coworking for Solospace.org, a metaverse platform. For more information on the various projects/initiatives he is involved in, visit davidhwalker.com.

Born and raised in the Lonestar State and a graduate of The University of Texas at Austin, Walker has spent most of his life there, but has also lived throughout Mexico, and is now settled in Prescott, Arizona with his wife and two sons. His personal mission is to help evolve outdated models of work into dynamic ecosystems of collaboration.
